# Day 2 Problem

Fun_Game
1) You have been handed a bag consisting of n = 20 items from which you can sample different items with respective utilities atmost 5 times
2) After each time u sample from the bag u can either chose to keep the item and end the game or replace it and sample again till ur draws are exhausted
3) Your score is the average utility of the items chosen across all rounds
4) The files environment.py, game.py are helper programs simulating the environment & the scoring
5) U need to implement ur agent which would make a decision on whether u stand by the item or replace and draw again 
6) The input & return values of the function has been commented in your_agent.py
